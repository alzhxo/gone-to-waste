Taking a deeper look at the scale and impact of food waste in the United States

Created by Alicia Z., Adeel C., and Haritha S. R. for INFO 247 Spring 2023

Food loss and waste in the United States is a huge problem

The United States is the 2nd largest generator of food loss and waste (FLW) in the world, both per capita and overall.

In the United States, 35% of all food produced for human consumption goes. unsold or uneaten.

While a small fraction of this surplus gets donated or recycled, the majority of it ends up wasted.
